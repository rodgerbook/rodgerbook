About Rodger

Throughout my career as a subconscious painter, my work has been directed by spirituality, color, and light as an Abstract Expressionist. At no time during the process of painting do I force my beliefs as to what the subject matter should pertain to. Every painting has a life of its own, and must be respected at all times until the painting's completion. As a painter I look beyond the initial plane of a painting to search for the unknown, grasp its place of origin, and accept that anything is possible if you are willing to look beyond what you initially see.

Therefore, every painting that is completed is never the same, and each painting has its own being and purpose. Skill is one thing, but ultimately vision is the true path to answer what you search for as a painter.

However, if you don't have the passion to see your journey through in your work as a painter, then all is lost anyway.
